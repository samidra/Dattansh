Our Approach

Data-Driven Insights

We begin with secondary data analysis to identify broad electoral trends. For instance, in Maharashtra, regional realities and concerns dominate elections, with no pan-Maharashtra issue taking center stage.

Grassroots Perspective

The identified trends and issues are validated and refined through discussions with key grassroots stakeholders, such as local leaders, community representatives, and experts.

Understanding Voter Choice Architecture

By combining data and field insights, we analyze how issues, social alliances, and narratives influence voter decisions. This helps us explore the voter choice architecture, including the motivations and factors shaping electoral behavior.

Seat-Level Analysis

All insights are synthesized to assess each electoral seat, offering a detailed understanding of its unique dynamics.

Ecosystem

"In today's fast-paced digital landscape, voters are constantly bombarded with information from various channels, such as social media, news outlets, and personal networks. This makes it crucial for political communications to be both targeted and effective. For example, a voter in a rural area might receive most of her information through local radio and community gatherings, whereas a young urban voter could rely on Instagram and Twitter for updates. At Dattansh, we understand the importance of reaching these diverse audiences with a strategic and adaptable approach."
